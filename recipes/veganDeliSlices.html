Vegan Deli Slices

A great! High protein recipe created by Natalie Mathews the "Fit Vegan Chef". I love these seitan these deli slices. I use these in my wraps, burritos, sandwiches, or even with a cheese and cracker snack!

Ingredients

10 servings
  1. 300g vital wheat gluten
  2. 125g unflavored pea protien
  3. 40g nutritional yeast
  4. 1 tbsp + 1 tsp disired seasoning (make sure it has salt)
  5. 8 cups of water (add more if needed)

Intructions

1) Preheat oven to 350Â°F, line a baking pan with parchment paper, and lightly spray it with non-stick spray.

2) Measure the dry ingredients with a kitchen scale and add them to a large mixing bowl. Mix.

3) In a small mixing bowl, combine the wet ingredients and slowly add to the dry mix. Mix with your hands until reaching a bread dough consistency. (3-5 minutes)

4) Knead the mixture and roll 4 logs. Place them on the baking pan.

5) Cover the baking pan and bake for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, remove the cover, flip, and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes.

6) Once Seitan is cooked, let it completely cool before slicing (1hr+).

Slice in a food processor or use a mandoline slicer.

7) Enjoy!
