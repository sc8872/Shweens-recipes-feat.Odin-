Lupin Waffles

Ingredients

1 servings
  1. 1/2 cup lupin flour
  2. 1 scoop plant protein of choice
  3. 2 teaspoons cacao powder
  4. 1 teaspoon flax meal
  5. 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  6. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  7. 1 cup water or plant-based milk

Intructions

1) Pre-heat waffle maker to the highest heat setting.

2) In a food processor combine lupin flour, plant protein, flax meal, cacao powder, baking powder, and baking soda - blend together.

3) Add water and process all the ingredients until they are well combined and form a batter.

4) Spray a little bit of coconut oil to waffle maker to prevent the waffles from sticking.

5) Pour batter into the hot waffle maker and heat waffles for 7-11 minutes or until desired consistency. Repeat the process until all the batter is gone.

6) Enjoy!
