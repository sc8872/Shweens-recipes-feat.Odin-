Simple Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

2 servings
  • 1 brick (396g) extra firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp (12g) nutritional yeast
  • 2 tsp seasoning of your choice

Intructions

1) Crumble the brick of tofu.

2) Heat a medium size non-stick pan over medium-high heat.

3) Add crumbled tofu to your pan and stir for 5 minutes.

4) Add nutritional yeast and seasoning of choice and stire for 30 more seconds.

5) Serve warm on its own, with ketchup or salsal, on toast, with tortillas, with potatos whatever your heart desires.
